Pre-deploy feature scope pinned. Five product features added to roadmap before GCP work:

2. Campaign-create wizard refactor with DMA targeting cards (REACH = live sum of selected display counts), filtering bids on selected markets.
3. Hardcoded "1 every 5 min" frequency line under REACH.
